Inspiration

Here I gathered things that make me happy and give me power to live.

Love

In any and all contexts, love is an amazing thing. The support and respect and magic that it provides never fails.

Community

When a group of people comes together because they like something, believe in something, support something or want to create positive change I am inspired and want to be part of something great as well.

Music

Great songs move people. The creativity and talent that it takes to make music amazes me in a wonderful way.

Moving my body

Physical activities bring clarity and calm. They help to quiet my racing, jumbled thoughts. They clear away the cobwebs so I can meet my ideas.

Dreams

I’m talking about the kind of dreams that people work towards in their life. Travel and careers and homes and creating things – seeing people go after their dreams is inspiring.

Beauty

I’m not talking about people as an example. I’m talking about the little things that are beautiful. Flowers and sunshine and the way nature presents pictures to us in real life that sometimes can’t be translated into photographs or paintings.

Graphic and web design

I could look at logos and beautiful websites all day.

Quotes from books

Words about truly important things in life - such as friendship, kindness, love - are so encouraging. They lift me up and even make me cry. Quotes that you read and get goosebumps.

Pinterest

I love browsing the site to see the incredible things people come up with. You can click on "Pinterest" and explore it.

Ocean

The smell of the ocean and the sound of the waves crashing onto the shore.
